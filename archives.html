BLACK BEAN DIP

We've a number of inquiries about the black bean dip that Charlie often serves with sunset margaritas on the deck.  It is made by his housekeeper Berta (or as many have taken to calling her The Enforcer).  Here's the recipe:

BERTA'S BLACK BEAN DIP

2 cans of black beans from can

1 chopped green pepper

1 chopped red pepper

1 small can of corn

1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Jalapeños to taste

1 large Spanish onion, chopped

16 oz. Sour cream

1 cup salsa

Rinse black beans. Put aside a small portion of onions to use for garnish.   Mix the rest of the onions, peppers, corn and jalapeños together.   In a serving bowl, put a layer of beans, then a layer of the onion/peppers/jalapeño mix. Then a thick layer of sour cream and repeat. Refrigerate overnight.   Top with salsa and chopped onions, garnish with cheese and a dollop of sour cream. Serve with tortilla chips.
